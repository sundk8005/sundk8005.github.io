Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Why can’t I see coordinates for this photo?
    A: Make sure the image you selected actually has GPS data embedded.
  • Q: Where are my photos stored?
    A: All photos remain on your device; GeoPix only saves local copies.
  • Q: How do I remove an entry from history?
    A: Go to “History,” swipe left on any entry, and tap “Delete.”
  • Q: Can I share my location data?
    A: Copy coordinates or address to your clipboard and paste it anywhere.
