  <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

  <ul>

    <li><strong>Q:</strong> Why can’t I see coordinates for this photo?

      <br /><strong>A:</strong> Make sure the image you selected actually has GPS data embedded. Some screenshots or imported images may not include location metadata.

    </li>

    <li><strong>Q:</strong> Where are my photos stored?

      <br /><strong>A:</strong> All photos remain on your device. GeoPix only saves local copies in its own database for history/timeline. We never upload your photos to a server.

    </li>

    <li><strong>Q:</strong> How do I remove an entry from history?

      <br /><strong>A:</strong> Go to “History,” swipe left on any entry, and tap “Delete.”

    </li>

    <li><strong>Q:</strong> Can I share my location data?

      <br /><strong>A:</strong> Currently there’s no direct “Share” button, but you can copy coordinates or address to your clipboard and paste it anywhere.

    </li>

  </ul>
