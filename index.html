Privacy Policy for GeoPix

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • GPS Coordinates: When you select a photo that has location metadata, GeoPix reads the embedded GPS data (latitude/longitude).
  • Images: Any image you pick remains on your device. GeoPix only stores a local copy (never on our servers).
  • Advertising Identifiers: We use Google AdMob for banner ads. AdMob may collect an advertising identifier (IDFA) or do device fingerprinting in its SDK. For more details, see Google AdMob Privacy.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • We reverse‐geocode your photo’s embedded GPS coordinates to display a human‐readable address.
  • Your coordinates and address are stored locally in Core Data on your device, so you can browse your photo history.
  • We show banner ads via Google AdMob; no personally identifiable photo data is sent to our servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your photos or location data with any third party. Google AdMob may serve ads using device identifiers, as described in their privacy policy.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version here with a new “Last updated” date.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the “Support” link on our website.
