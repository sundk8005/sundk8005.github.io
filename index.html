  <h1>Privacy Policy for GeoPix</h1>

  <p>Last updated: May 2025</p>


  <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>

  <ul>

    <li><strong>GPS Coordinates:</strong> When you select a photo that has location metadata, GeoPix reads the embedded GPS data (latitude/longitude).</li>

    <li><strong>Images:</strong> Any image you pick remains on your device. GeoPix only stores a local copy (never on our servers).</li>

    <li><strong>Advertising Identifiers:</strong> We use Google AdMob for banner ads. AdMob may collect an advertising identifier (IDFA) or do device fingerprinting in its SDK. For more details, see 

      <a href="https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites">Google AdMob Privacy</a>.

    </li>

  </ul>


  <h2>2. How We Use Your Data</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>We reverse‐geocode your photo’s embedded GPS coordinates to display a human‐readable address.</li>

    <li>Your coordinates and address are stored locally in Core Data on your device, so you can browse your photo history.</li>

    <li>We show banner ads via Google AdMob; no personally identifiable photo data is sent to our servers.</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>3. Data Sharing</h2>

  <p>We do not share your photos or location data with any third party. Google AdMob may serve ads using device identifiers, as described in their privacy policy.</p>


  <h2>4. Changes to This Policy</h2>

  <p>We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version here with a new “Last updated” date.</p>


  <hr />

  <p>If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the “Support” link on our website.</p>
