Privacy Policy for GeoPix

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • GPS Coordinates: When you select a photo with location metadata, GeoPix reads the embedded latitude/longitude.
  • Images: Any image you pick remains only on your device. GeoPix stores a local copy in Core Data but never uploads your photos to a server.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We show banner ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect an advertising identifier (IDFA) or perform device fingerprinting in its SDK; see Google AdMob Privacy for details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • We reverse‑geocode your photo’s embedded GPS to display a human‑readable address.
  • Your coordinates and addresses are stored locally so you can browse your history.
  • No personal photo data is ever sent to our servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your photos or location data with any third party. Google AdMob delivers ads using device identifiers as per their own privacy policy.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.

If you have questions, please visit our Support page or email support@your-domain.com.
